Questions and Answers

  • How much does this cost?

    Admission is completely free. Food, workspace, and WiFi are provided at no cost.

  • Do I need to come with a team?

    No, teams will be formed the morning of the event.

  • How big are teams?

    You may work individually, or in a group of up to five total people.

  • What should I bring?

    Bring a photo ID for check-in, a laptop, chargers, and anything else you may need for the hackathon
